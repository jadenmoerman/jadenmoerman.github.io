The History of Nintendo Consoles

Nintendo is a company with a long and storied past. Nintendo was founded in 1889 and their original buisness was producing hand-made playing cards. In 1966, with buisness struggling, they expanded into toys before expanding further into game consoles. This website is an online museum of the all game consoles that Nintendo has made since 1972.
